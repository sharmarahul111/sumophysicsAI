# sumophysicsAI
Two wrestler AIs fight for the staying inside the circle

At first I was skeptical of my AI agents. By looking at their playing style among themselved, I thought they aren't learning anything. Only when I tried to play with one of the top AI myself, I realized that they had learned a lot and maybe were playing optimum strategy from both sides and a human player may have a very rare chance to win against them.
